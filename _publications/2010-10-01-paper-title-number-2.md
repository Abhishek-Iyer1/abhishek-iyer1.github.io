excerpt: 'In this work, we present Eformer - Edge enhance-
ment based transformer, a novel architecture that builds
an encoder-decoder network using transformer blocks for
medical image denoising. Non-overlapping window-based
self-attention is used in the transformer block that reduces
computational requirements. This work further incorpo-
rates learnable Sobel-Feldman operators to enhance edges
in the image and propose an effective way to concatenate
them in the intermediate layers of our architecture. The ex-
perimental analysis is conducted by comparing determin-
istic learning and residual learning for the task of medi-
cal image denoising. To defend the effectiveness of our ap-
proach, our model is evaluated on the AAPM-Mayo Clinic
Low-Dose CT Grand Challenge Dataset and achieves state-
of-the-art performance, i.e., 43.487 PSNR, 0.0067 RMSE,
and 0.9861 SSIM. We believe that our work will encourage
more research in transformer-based architectures for medi-
cal image denoising using residual learning'
